01 — About

A bit about me.

I'm an automation engineer who's spent the past 2+ years at Kore.ai making sure software does what it's supposed to do — reliably, at scale, and without someone clicking through every screen manually.

My work sits at the intersection of test engineering and AI. I've built frameworks using Selenium, Playwright, and Karate, and more recently I've been deep in LLM testing — designing validation utilities for chatbot responses and optimizing prompts to improve accuracy.

I care about clean automation that actually saves time, CI/CD pipelines that give teams confidence to ship, and writing code that the next person can understand.
